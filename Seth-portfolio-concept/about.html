My interest in photography started with capturing concerts and other local events.

Between being on stage with drumsticks in my hands, and off stage with a camera, I filled my early portfolio with the chaos of the live music night life. I felt and still feel like starting local artists needed that kind of content more than most and I knew I would be at those shows anyway and if you’re a new photographer you’re always the douchebag that takes your camera everywhere with you, and I was no exception.

The Party After w/Sam Hayes of Gallivant
Jana Stone | Fitness Model and Owner of The Metals Bar

Eventually I got connected to shooting with models to add to my portrait portfolio.

My photography hobby was starting to turn into a more serious career ambition and I wanted to branch out widen the kind of work I could make myself available for. I got connected with a local fitness model and I had the pleasure of having that connection lead to a lot of different style challenges!

Now I use photography to create images that invoke confidence for the subjects, and provoke thought for the viewers.

Whether its a visceral protest of American ignorance, a tasteful boudoir shot, or hell, maybe even a wedding, I would love to create something beautiful with you!
