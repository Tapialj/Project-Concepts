Art With A Message

When I struggle to vocalize the way I feel about something, I try like hell to get a good photo out of it. Seeing it laid out on camera normally helps me to get my thoughts together enough to explain these feelings better.

A Climate Change Story

Every day we further injure the planet, is another day closer to our own ends.

It's the execution of us all, but we are pulling the trigger.

Blood and Foundations

The last several years haven’t exactly been the best example of race relations in this country. I noticed that the people who were complaining generally about athletes kneeling for the National Anthem, seemed to have a disconnect that I don’t know how to effectively point out.

It’s hard to tell someone their actions or words are racist without inciting severe anger, and once someone has gotten angry, the chances of a reasonable debate are gone. The American flag flies above a foundation that was built by slaves, and is bloodied by the injustices that still permeate the systems we all depend on today. To top it off, to have white people telling them to stand for that flag or lose their job is just yet another injustice to add to a long list.

Artificial Colors

One of the worst things about social media is the shaping of young women’s minds that it is now responsible for.

Before they even have the chance to learn who they are or who they want to be, they are inundated with unavoidable trends, impossible beauty standards and the worst examples of mental health role models that can be imagined.

This leads into an adulthood of self dissatisfaction, depression and anxiety that invades every aspect of their lives. But they are still expected to put a happy face on and show the vibrance of their lives every day, while the only color they get to keep behind the scenes is orange.

Infallible

Yes. The fire is real, and yes it is a Bible.

Shock value for the sake of shock value is not at all the intention here. Religion is the basis of a lot of horrible behavior nowadays, and while I know that the majority of religious people and services are of high moral value and necessity, the issues in the world that religion “justifies” through misinterpretations and cherry pickings of the Bible are inexcusable.

When someone arguing using the latter is backed into a corner, they often use the first amendment’s guarantee of freedom of religion as the groundwork for believing that their religion is infallible.

The first amendment proves that isn’t true, and makes this image possible.

Speak no peace

Increasingly, I am seeing some ugly responses to poc’s calling for basic human rights and justice for their peers. Too often are the actions of a few, poisoning peaceful demonstrations and souring the message that black Americans are trying to voice.

Black history is relegated to being taught only over the course of the shortest month of the year. Juneteenth become a federal holiday, and was subsequently banned from being taught in schools in several states.

It seems that America is all for free speech, until the hard truths start getting told.
